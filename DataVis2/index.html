World Human Health & Economy

Data Visualization 2 by Marc Vitas

Introducing a visualisation containing data from the "Global Country Information Dataset 2023"

which is a comprehensive collection of vital statistics and indicators that offer insights into the state of our global health. This a valuable resource that provides a view of the factors influencing public health and prosperity worldwide. Dive into this data visualisation to uncover trends and opportunities for improving the well-being of populations across the globe. Whether you're a skeptic, or anyone passionate about global health, this visualisation is your key to understanding and addressing critical health factors on a global scale.

Life Expectancy in 2023

This map visualizes the Infant Mortality Rate per 1000 people in the year 2023 across different countries. The data is represented using a geographical map

Life Expectancy vs Infant Mortality

This interactive chart displays the relationship between Life Expectancy and Infant mortality for various countries. You can adjust the population threshold using the slider above to filter countries by population. The size of each circle represents the population of a country, and you can hover over the circles to view additional information.
